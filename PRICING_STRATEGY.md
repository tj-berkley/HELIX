### Why Customers Will Pay

1. **Value Stacking:** Replaces 15+ tools = saves $1,000+/mo
2. **ROI Focus:** "Save 10 hours/week" = $2,000/mo in time value
3. **All-in-One:** Eliminates tool switching and integration costs
4. **HELIX AI:** Intelligent automation feels premium and futuristic
5. **Industry-Specific:** Tailored for doctors, lawyers, real estate, etc.
6. **Trial Period:** 14 days free removes purchase friction
7. **Social Proof:** Case studies showing 300%+ ROI

- Show "replaces $1,184/mo in tools" before pricing
